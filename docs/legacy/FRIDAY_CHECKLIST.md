### IF SOMETHING BREAKS DURING DEMO

**Scenario 1: Demo Won't Load**
- Stay calm, DON'T panic
- Say: "Let me show you a screenshot instead - this is the output"
- Show backup image
- Continue with confidence

**Scenario 2: Numbers Look Different**
- Say: "The algorithm considers multiple factors. This variation is expected."
- Continue forward

**Scenario 3: Someone Asks Technical Question**
- If you know: Answer confidently
- If you don't: "Great question. Let me get that detail for you."
- Move forward (don't get stuck)

**Scenario 4: You Stumble on Words**
- Pause, take breath, restart sentence
- They won't notice (everyone does it)
- Keep going with confidence
